// Who We Are

A Recognised Voice for Lab Employees

BISLEA is the registered employees' association for the laboratory staff of the Bureau of Indian Standards, recognized by BIS Management since 2019.

We operate under the Societies Registration Act, 1860 and function in accordance with the Government of India's CCS RSA Rules, 1993.

This website is maintained by current office bearers for transparency and accessibility of all relevant documents, circulars, and communications.
